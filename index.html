Product History

AirHeads Xtremes and AirHeads Xtremes bites were both released in 2012. They are the sour spin off of the original Airheads candy, which were created in 1985. The 1985 AirHeads didn't have a specific flavor, they just came in a red package. This was because some parts of the country preferred cherry and some preferred strawberry so keeping it in a red package appealed to both crowds.

XTREME FLAVOR

Airheads Xtremes are a mix between the sweet fruit flavors Airheads are known for and Xtreme sourness. They come in two forms, sour bites and sour ropes. There are two flavors, the original rainbow flavor and the blue raspberry flavor. This is a delicious candy you definiety want to try.

Perfetti Van Melle

Airheads are a Perfetti Van Melle product.Perfetti Van Melle also makes other products such as Mentos and Chupa Chups lolipos. They are the largest distributer of chewing gum. Perfetti and Van Melle used to two different companies but in 2003 they merged into one. The Van Melle company was started in 1900 when Izaak Van Melle turned his bakery which was passed onto him from his grandfather into a candy store.
